Ghostlight for the solo developer

Ten minutes from clone to an agent driving your real browser, plus the optional personal safety rails. Everything on this page is free, forever, with no key and no registration.

Read the guide

What you get

Your AI agent gets a real browser: your cookies, your logins, your tabs. Fourteen tools — navigate, click, type, screenshot, read the page — at byte-parity with the schemas the model was trained on.

By default Ghostlight is all-open: no policy, no restrictions, no audit. Governance is an overlay you opt into later, one setting at a time.

drag across me

The agent works inside its own tab group, labelled with a ghost, so its activity stays visually separate from yours.

Every effect here is hidden from the agent's own screenshots — the model's image stays clean; these are for the human watching.
