# Support

Ghostlight is maintained by one person. Public questions are welcome, but response times are best
effort unless a separate agreement says otherwise.

Use the narrowest appropriate lane:

- Reproducible defects and installation friction: [GitHub Issues](../../issues).
- Questions, ideas, feature requests, policy patterns, and show-and-tell: [GitHub Discussions](../../discussions).
- Suspected vulnerabilities: follow [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md) and report privately.
- Licensing or a matter that cannot be public: email hello@sylin.org.

Before sharing diagnostics, remove credentials, cookies, private page content, audit payloads,
identities, and personal paths. `ghostlight doctor` is the best first diagnostic for installation
and connection problems.

[CONTRIBUTING.md](CONTRIBUTING.md) explains how requests are evaluated and how to prepare a code
contribution. [MAINTENANCE.md](MAINTENANCE.md) explains continuity, maintainer capacity, and single
points of failure.
